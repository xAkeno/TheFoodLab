MilkTea

Milk tea is a popular, refreshing beverage that blends tea with milk, creating a creamy and smooth drink that can be served hot or cold. Originating from various cultures, milk tea has many forms, from classic British tea with milk to Asian varieties like bubble tea, which includes chewy tapioca pearls. With endless flavor options and add-ins like boba, jellies, or fruit, milk tea has become a go-to choice for tea lovers seeking both flavor and texture.
